As we begin 2023 on a note of new hope and promise, the Government of His Majesty Sultan Haitham Bin Tarik continues to make rapid strides in improving the investment climate in the Sultanate. The latest statistics show that over 800,000 applications were submitted through the Invest Easy portal, with about 765,000 licenses being issued automatically from April 2021 when the service was launched till December 2022. Meanwhile, the Oman Investment Authority has stated that in 2023, it will spend RO 1.9 bn on about 65 projects spread across logistics, energy, communications and IT, mining, services, food and fisheries sectors. Encouragingly for the Sultanate’s economy, the preliminary financial results for 2022 shows that Oman is set to record its first budget surplus since 2013 of RO 1.15 billion.
